AgriSmart: Intelligent Technologies for Sustainable and Educational Agriculture

The AgriSmart project revolutionizes agriculture in Valle del Cauca by integrating a patented biosensor to monitor CO2 and other environmental signals.

Temperature (°C)

Ambient temperature in degrees Celsius. Suggested range: 20°C to 30°C. Affects plant growth and survival rate.

Humidity (%)

Relative humidity percentage in the environment. Suggested range: 40% to 70%. Humidity is crucial for plant health.

Light (Lux)

Light intensity measured in lumens per square meter. Suggested range: 3000 to 7000 Lux. Influences photosynthesis and plant growth.

Soil pH

pH level of the soil where plants are growing. Suggested range: 6.0 to 7.5. A pH is essential for nutrient absorption.

Nutrients (ppm)

Nutrient amounts in parts per million. Suggested range: 100 to 250 ppm. Nutrients are essential for plant growth.

CO2 concentration

Concentration of carbon dioxide in the environment. Suggested range: 0.0 to 1.0. CO2 is necessary for photosynthesis.

Project Description

Agriculture in the Valle del Cauca, Colombia, is vital due to its favorable climate and rich tradition, with legumes playing a key role in soil fertility and crop rotation. Farmers face challenges in managing these crops efficiently and sustainably. To address this, an intelligent platform using AI, 3D visualizations, and augmented reality (AR) is proposed. This system will monitor real-time data on various environmental factors, using machine learning to predict plant health and survival. AR will allow farmers to interact with 3D crop models and receive personalized recommendations, enhancing decision-making and crop management.

"An intelligent platform using AI and AR will enhance legume crop management in the Valle del Cauca, Colombia."

  • Giovanny Hidalgo, PhD. Artificial Intelligence

Objectives

Develop an intelligent interactive platform based on artificial intelligence and augmented reality to support the management and sustainability of legume crops in the Cauca Valley, Colombia.

Data model

Design a model that allows collecting and storing environmental variables from different data sources.

Artificial intelligence

Implement artificial intelligence computational techniques to make predictions and recommendations for legume plants based on the data collected.

Web development

Develop a web platform that integrates augmented reality (AR) and artificial intelligence (AI) computational techniques to provide farmers with real-time tools and data on the management of their crops.

Testing

Validate the Leguimonosa case study on the developed web platform.
